# SFHS IRON - FUTURE ENGINEERS CATEGORY
This repository contains the development of our SPIKE Prime-based autonomous robot for the Future Engineers category of the 25th Philippine Robotics Olympiad (2026), designed as a reliable sensor-driven without camera vision.

This is the official repository of SFHS IRON, representing the San Francisco High School in the 25th Philippine Robotics Olympiad. Our robot is designed as a fully autonomous car that operates without the use of a camera, relying entirely on sensor-based navigation and precise control algorithms.
